Covid-19 Rates by County in the US
December 2020, Sources: ACS and NY Times

Hover over a state!

This is an interactive choropleth webmap showcasing Covid-19 rates by county. Rates are calculated using Covid-19 case data from the NY Times and ACS population data (sources found in subtitle) to measure the number of Covid-19 cases per 1,000 people in each county. As seen on the map, most of the US county Covid-19 rates fall between 50-100 cases per 1,000 people.
